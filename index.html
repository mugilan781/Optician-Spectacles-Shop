Precision Lens Guide

Choose the Right Lens for Your Eyes

Our qualified optometrists help you select the perfect lens technology — matched precisely to your prescription, lifestyle, and visual demands.

Single Vision Lenses

One focal point for clear, comfortable vision throughout the lens. Ideal for distance, reading, or digital screen use.

  • ✓ Corrects near or distance vision
  • ✓ Most affordable lens type
  • ✓ Available in all materials
  • ✓ Starting from ₹999

HD Freeform Progressive

Digital freeform surfacing for seamless distance, intermediate, and near vision — all in one precision lens.

  • ✓ Three vision zones in one lens
  • ✓ Wider reading channel
  • ✓ German freeform surfacing
  • ✓ Starting from ₹3,999

Anti-Blue HEV Filter Lenses

Vacuum-deposited AR coating filtering 415–455nm high-energy visible wavelengths from screens.

  • ✓ Reduces digital eye strain
  • ✓ Improves sleep quality
  • ✓ Anti-reflective + scratch coating
  • ✓ Starting from ₹1,999

Photochromic Transition Lenses

Automatically adapt to ambient UV light — fully clear indoors, dark outside in seconds.

  • ✓ Adapts to all light conditions
  • ✓ UV400 protection built-in
  • ✓ No separate sunglasses needed
  • ✓ Starting from ₹4,499
