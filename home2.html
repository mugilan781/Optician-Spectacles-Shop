A comprehensive eye test at OptiLux typically takes 30–45 minutes. This includes the full refraction assessment, eye health check, glaucoma pressure test, and a discussion of your results and options.
We strongly recommend booking an appointment online or by phone to ensure your preferred time slot is available. However, we do accept walk-ins based on availability.
Adults should get a comprehensive eye test every 2 years. Children and those with existing eye conditions should be tested annually. If you notice any sudden vision changes, book immediately.
