About OptiLux

18 years of commitment to optical precision. Meet the founders, our qualified team, and the laboratory behind our bespoke frames.

Our Story

Bespoke Fitting & Clinical Care

OptiLux was founded in 2008 by Dr. Suresh Iyer, a qualified optometrist who believed that visual acuity diagnostics and custom-fitted frame tailoring should be treated with the highest precision. Starting as a boutique consultation clinic, we have expanded to serve over 15,000 satisfied patients across the region.

Every frame is tailored to the patient's visual field, inter-pupillary distance, and facial morphology. We pair the latest lenses with premium titanium and custom acetate materials sourced worldwide.
