About OptiLux

18 years of passion for perfect vision. Meet the people and story behind your favourite eyewear destination.

Our Story

From a Single Store
to a Trusted Brand

OptiLux was founded in 2008 by Dr. Suresh Iyer, a passionate optometrist who believed that clear vision shouldn't be a luxury — it should be a right. Starting with a small practice in Koramangala, we have grown into one of Bangalore's most trusted eyewear destinations.

Over the years, we've combined the latest lens technology with a curated selection of the world's finest frames to serve over 15,000 satisfied customers. Our philosophy has never changed: exceptional eye care, honest advice, and a frame for every face.

Today, with three premium stores across Bangalore and a team of 25+ qualified professionals, we continue to put your vision first — always.
