Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Effective Date: 1 July 2026 · Version 3.1

This Privacy Policy describes how OptiLux Private Limited ("OptiLux", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our services, visit our stores, or interact with our website and mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users and customers. The types of information we collect include:

  • Personal Identification: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, and address
  • Health & Medical Information: Eye prescriptions, eye health history, medical conditions relevant to vision care
  • Payment Information: Billing address, payment method details (we do not store full card numbers)
  • Device & Usage Information: IP address, browser type, device identifiers, usage logs, and analytics data
  • Communications: Messages you send us via email, contact forms, or WhatsApp

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our eyewear and eye care services
  • Process appointments, orders, and payments
  • Store and manage your eye prescriptions and health records
  • Send appointment reminders, order confirmations, and service updates
  • Send marketing communications (only with your consent)
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations
  • Improve our website and store experience through analytics

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it only in limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties who assist with payment processing, appointment software, email services, and analytics
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or regulatory authority
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • With Your Consent: For any other purpose with your explicit consent

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience. We use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly
  • Analytics Cookies: Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site
  • Marketing Cookies: To show relevant advertisements (with your consent)
  • Preference Cookies: To remember your theme and language settings

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time through your browser settings or by using our Cookie Preference Centre below.

5. Medical & Health Data

We understand that your eye health information is particularly sensitive. We apply the highest level of protection to all medical data, including your prescriptions and examination results. This data is:

  • Encrypted at rest and in transit using AES-256 encryption
  • Accessible only to qualified healthcare professionals involved in your care
  • Retained for a minimum of 7 years as required by Indian medical records regulations
  • Never shared with third parties without your explicit written consent, except as required by law

6. Your Data Rights

Under applicable data protection laws (including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023), you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing for marketing purposes
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Withdraw previously given consent at any time

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including SSL/TLS encryption, regular security audits, access controls, and staff training. Our systems are ISO 27001-aligned and we conduct annual penetration testing.

8. Children's Privacy

We do collect information about children as part of our paediatric eye care services. For children under 18, we require verifiable parental consent before collecting personal data. Parents may request access to, correction of, or deletion of their child's records at any time.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email and by posting a prominent notice on our website. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
